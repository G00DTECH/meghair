About Me

I'm a 17 year hair veteran from western Maine, first learning the fundamentals of hair in Boston on the reputable Newbury Street. I then returned to my home state for 10 years where I was fortunate enough to build steadfast relationships with my clients.

My Specialties

My approach to hairstyling is deeply influenced by the Vidal Sassoon philosophy of precision cutting and personalized style.

  • Precision Cuts
  • Geometric Shapes
  • Personalized Styling
  • Color Techniques
  • Hair Care Education

Philosophy

"It's the cut that counts." - Vidal Sassoon

I believe in creating haircuts that enhance my clients' natural features and fit their lifestyle. Every cut is a collaboration between stylist and client, resulting in a personalized look that's both beautiful and manageable.
